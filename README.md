# ☀️ Solar Energy Performance Analysis

## 📊 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive analysis of global solar energy performance using Power BI and MySQL. It focuses on financial performance, operational efficiency, and environmental sustainability to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🔧 Tools & Technologies

* Power BI
* MySQL
* Data Analysis (EDA)

---

## 📈 Key Insights

### 💰 Financial Performance & Investment Decisions

* Middle East and Africa show the highest investment efficiency
* Cities like Phoenix and Cairo have the highest ROI (~17%)
* Europe shows comparatively lower ROI rankings
* Payback risk varies across regions

---

### ⚙️ Production & Operational Performance

* Asia leads in total installations (over 1.5M units)
* Europe and Asia dominate annual energy production
* Strong correlation between sunlight hours and energy production
* Countries like Egypt and Israel show high average production

---

### 🌱 Sustainability & Environmental Impact

* United States and India contribute the highest CO₂ reduction
* Higher solar viability scores lead to higher ROI
* Strong relationship between production and environmental benefits
* Cities like Phoenix and Cairo lead in sustainability metrics

---

## 📊 Dashboard Overview

### Financial Dashboard

Analysis of ROI, investment efficiency, pricing trends, and payback risk across regions and cities.

### Production Dashboard

Insights into annual production, sunlight impact, installations by region, and operational efficiency.

### Sustainability Dashboard

Evaluation of CO₂ reduction, solar viability scores, and environmental impact across regions.

## 🚀 Conclusion

The solar energy sector demonstrates strong growth potential with increasing adoption and environmental benefits. Regions with high solar viability and investment efficiency offer the best opportunities for maximizing returns. This project highlights how data-driven analysis can support strategic investment and sustainability decisions.
